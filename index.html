Questions

The ones you're
already thinking.

Does Novu record my meetings?▾
Novu transcribes audio in real time but does not store permanent recordings. The transcript is processed to generate your summary and then deleted. You get the intelligence, not a surveillance archive.
Do other participants know Novu is there?▾
Yes, and that's intentional. Novu joins as a visible participant so everyone knows the call is being summarized. We believe transparency builds more trust than hidden recording. You can also enable a meeting-start announcement.
What languages does it support?▾
Novu currently works best in English, with strong support for Spanish, French, German, and Portuguese. We're actively expanding language coverage — if you need a specific language, let us know.
Can I use Novu with Microsoft Teams?▾
Microsoft Teams support is in active development and will be available for all Pro and Team subscribers by Q4. Free users will get access shortly after. You can join our waitlist in settings to be notified first.
Is my data secure?▾
All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We are SOC 2 Type II compliant and do not use your meeting content to train any AI models. For Team plans, you get dedicated data isolation and can choose your data residency region.
What happens when I hit my free meeting limit?▾
You'll get a notification when you're approaching your 5-meeting limit. Once you hit it, Novu will stop joining new calls until the next month — or until you upgrade to Pro. We never charge you automatically without your confirmation.
